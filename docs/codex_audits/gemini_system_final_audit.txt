- **Recommendation:** Update `lib/cli_adapter.sh` to include `gemini` in all Python inline tuples and add an explicit `gemini` case to `build_cli_command`.

### **Axis 2: Watcher Chain Reliability**
**Status: [YELLOW]**
- **Findings:** The "read-back verify" logic in `hakudokai_fukuincho_poll.py` and the 3-stage failsafe in `hakudokai_watchdog.sh` are highly robust. However, the **`shogun` (fukuincho) agent is omitted** from the `INBOX_AGENTS` list in the watchdog and startup scripts. Since the Shogun is the primary destination for clinical directives, this creates a reliability gap for the system's most critical agent.
- **Recommendation:** Add `shogun` to the `INBOX_AGENTS` list in `hakudokai_watchdog.sh` and `hakudokai_start_watchers.sh`.
